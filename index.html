Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Key Point: DebtPilot AI stores all your financial data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial information on any server.

1. Information We Collect

DebtPilot AI collects the following information that you voluntarily provide within the app:

  • Your name and email address (for display purposes only)
  • Financial data: monthly income, loan details, investment values, savings goals
  • Chat messages sent to the AI advisor feature

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All financial data is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service.

Important: If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted. We recommend exporting your data to CSV before uninstalling.

3. Third-Party Services

The AI Advisor feature uses Google's Gemini API to generate financial recommendations. When you use this feature:

  • Your financial context (income, loan details, investment summary) is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized advice.
  • This data is processed according to Google's AI Terms of Service.
  • We do not store conversation history on any external server.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All data remains on-device in an encrypted application sandbox.
  • API communications use HTTPS encryption.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in the app.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export all your data as a CSV file at any time from the Profile screen.
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any loan, investment, or goal within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

DebtPilot AI is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
